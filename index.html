University Of Southern Mississippi

Research Assistant September 2016 - May 2018

Remote sensing data gives essential and critical information for detecting any object, a place, change detection and land cover of selected area of interest. Runway detection is an important topic because if it's applications in military and civil aviation fields. I gathered the latest data from NASA and implemented different clustering methods. I was fortunate enough to share my research to NASA team and even give a presentation in 2018 Maths & Science event.
